Humble Web


Harsha Suryanarayana, known as HUMBLEFOOL into the coding community, and as "Lord" by his friends and family, was the best coder in India. He was one of the first red coders (TopCoder) from the country and remained at the zenith until his last SRM. Contrary to the convention, he went on making Dynamic Programming his strongest skill. He was the TCO finalist twice and GCJ once. Humblefool was a man of passion. He gave up his job in Microsoft only to train his college team for ACM ICPC.

Inspired by Harsha’s passion, we here at TechniiC, invoke passionate coders from all the colleges across Jabalpur to come up and participate in this Web Development Competion, "HumbleWeb”. This competition is being conducted by the technical club, TechniiC in association with Udgam. Focusing on the begginers and on those who want to start learning web development we are inviting applications for a portfolio site. The participants are going to work single-handedly over the task, i.e., to create a pseudo-portfolio site, with single or multiple pages. This competition will be judged by the technical team of the Club, and the winners will be awarded exciting prices worth over 2k.
